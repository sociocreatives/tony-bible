About Bibble Art Wallpaper HD

An app that allows you to carry your Christian faith in beautiful wallpaper, drawn from bible scene paintings and Christian art general.

The categories we have covers the whole bible from Genesis to Revelation (Old to the New testaments). They include the following:

Testament (OT): Creation and Fall ; Noah- the flood ; Moses- the exodus ; King David ; Bible women etc

New Testament (NT): Birth of Christ; Passion of Christ ; Teachings of Jesus; Parables of Jesus etc

Each painting wallpaper has summary relevant bible verses, and a link to the full verses for your reflection. All paintings can be downloaded for your offline viewing and used as wallpaper.

We recommend the SLIDE SHOW, which allows you to go through categories (including favourites), almost as if watching a movie/picture show of the paintings. Look for the gear button to adjust the speed of the slide show.

Our app is generally free, with LIMITED access to categories (both NT and OT); for FULL access, a tiny yearly subscription is charged. We use the funds for app maintenance and the growth of more images and categories.

We do not allow for advertising due to its invasive nature; so both the free and paid versions are adverts free.

If you find the app a blessing, please bless others by sharing. We aim to keep adding wallpaper as we discover more faith building paintings and Christian images in general, so keep checking in.
